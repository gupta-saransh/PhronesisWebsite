PHRONESIS'19

Annual Literature Festival of MAIT

Phronesis is a humongous literary gala that recognizes the constructive power of words to foster learning ,inspiration and to provoke conversations that matter. Every year this confluence of creativity, cognizance and calibre experiences a footfall of over tens of thousands and is abuzz with activity that pulsates with energy, fuelled by the enthusiasm of students across universities and colleges in Delhi and beyond.

This March we bring to you a vast multitude of events with our two-day literary extravaganza wherein you all will not only have a gala time and an experience of a lifetime but shall also stand to earn recognition and win cash and other exciting prizes!
