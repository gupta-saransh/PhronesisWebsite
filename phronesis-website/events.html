JOTTINGS JENGA


Ever wondered how the shooting stars could carry the burden of all our wishes and yet soar across the sky weightlessly?

Ever admired the fidelity of the ocean waves as they refuse to stop kissing the shore, indifferent to the countless times they have been sent away ?

If delineating the idiosyncrasies of your characters is your forté and conjuring alternate universes your escapade, then we at Literary Umbrella, the official literary society of MAIT promise to be all ears (and eyes).

Voila! Phronesis'19 hereby invites all the shower thinkers, 3 am musers, amateur writers and prudent contemplators to JOTTINGS JENGA, an event specifically curated to celebrate your inner raconteur and test your proficiency in writing.

And as every good thing comes with a set of rules, so does this one!

  1. Since the water does not flow until the faucet is turned on, prompts will be provided on the spot to stimulate your grey cells.
  2. We firmly believe in the freedom of expression, hence the event will be bilingual.
  3. Minimum word limit is 200. We are optimistic that you will surpass that.
  4. The event has been curated to test your creativity, not the internet's. Kindly refrain from using phones/electronic media.
  5. Participants are requested to reach the venue well in advance to witness the full event in all its glory.

    6. Let the world burn through you! Throw the prism light, white hot, on blank paper.

ElectNext

“Small differences in a system of great power can have enormous consequences.”

In a presidential campaign, you can't lie. You can't hide who you are and what you want, or the kind of President you'll make. Our recently established country “LitUm” is looking for a President, someone who understands the ideals of a democracy and is willing to tackle the challenges faced by a new country.

Phronesis’19 brings to you ElectNext , a one-of-its-kind event that let's you experience the thrill and the pressure of convincing an audience why you deserve to be the President.

The people in this Presidential race will be from various fields (fictional as well as non-fictional, mind you!). Each participant will be assigned a personality and he or she has to convince why the personality assigned to them deserves to be the President.

The participants will be divided in groups on the basis of their genre. Those who qualify the first round will get to participate in the second, the final round.

Participants must follow these guidelines:

  1. Individual Participation.
  2. Each speaker will be given 2.5 minutes. Marks will be deducted for not adhering to the time limit.
  3. Use of slang or any inappropriate language will be grounds for disqualification
  4. All arguments shall be delivered in English. Use of any other language will lead to deduction of marks.
  5. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
